Personal pages

I'm a Cyber Security enthusiast and researcher.

I'm currently working as the Cyber Security Lead at Global Shares. I am a trusted advisor to C-suite management for multi-year, global, Cyber Security strategic initiatives and programs. I was formerly the Principal Cyber Security Engineer for the organisation.

Previously, I worked as a Senior Cyber Security Engineer at Johnson Controls (JCI) Fortune 500, where I specialized in Product Security and Enterprise Application Security.

Prior to this, I was a Post-Doctoral Researcher (Cyber Security) at the Mobile and Internet Systems Laboratory (MISL), Department of Computer Science, University College Cork (UCC), Ireland. I worked as part of the CHIST-ERA DYPOSIT project. My research was directed by Dr. Simon Foley, and focused primarily on autonomic security control reconfiguration, and the problem of large, shared Cyber-Physical System infrastructures under attack.

I have served as a nominated peer-reviewer of scientific publications for the European Symposium on Research in Computer Security (ESORICS).

I hold a Ph.D in Cyber Security from the Department of Computer Science in UCC. I also hold an M.Sc. in Software and Systems for Mobile Networks, and a B.Sc. (Hons.) in Computer Science, both awarded from University College Cork.

My current research interests include pen-testing automation and autonomic security control reconfiguration, dynamic policy frameworks, network and host-based access controls, mobile security, cloud security and cyber-physical - including ICS and IoT security.

I am also a firm believer in the truth of mathematics and formal modeling. My Erdős number is 5 ☺

I love hearing from people with similar interests, so if you are a like-minded individual then feel free to get in-touch! Contact details included in page footer.
